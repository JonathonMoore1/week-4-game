Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are a few crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will be adding to your total score.


You win the game by matching the total score to the random number, and lose if your total score exceeds the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal will change.
